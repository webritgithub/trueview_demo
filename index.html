About Us

Classic RMC stands for Ready Mix Concrete and just like our product, we are ready to take up any challenge with strong and concrete foundation. Classic RMC started in the year 2013 and since then, we haven’t looked back. From our first dedicated plant in Mumbai to contributing to the bullet train network development, these 10 years are packed with achievements and milestones.
Customized solutions for all your concrete needs” are a line that holds true for us. We don’t just sell concrete; we deliver results that contribute in the development of the nation.
